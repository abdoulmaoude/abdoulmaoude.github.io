Biography

Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a Postdoctoral researcher at Concordia University. My research interest relate to computational statistics and machine learning technics applying in finance. More precisely, I am interested in hidden Markov models, statistical inference for regime-switching models, stochastic volatility models, portfolio optimization, Hedging and reinforcement learning. My projects also involve using of Bayesian models, Monte-Carlo methods and simulations.

I received my PhD in Statistics from Université de Montréal in 2022.
